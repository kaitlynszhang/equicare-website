Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Don't see yours? Feel free to reach out!

How quickly do you respond to messages?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Can I schedule a consultation about my horse's specific needs?

While we can't provide veterinary advice, we're happy to discuss how our tools and research might be relevant to your horse's situation. Please mention this in your message and we'll schedule a call.

Do you offer partnerships with veterinarians or equine professionals?

Yes! We're always interested in collaborating with equine professionals. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form and tell us about your practice or organization.

Can I contribute to your research or share my horse's data?

We'd love to hear from horse owners who are interested in contributing to our research. Please select "Research Inquiry" and we'll share information about current studies and participation opportunities.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and will never share your contact information or personal details without your explicit consent. Your data is used only to respond to your inquiry and provide updates if you opt in.
